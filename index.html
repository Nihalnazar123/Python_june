Biriyani

Price : 130.Rs

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of the Indian subcontinent. It is made with Indian spices, rice, and usually some type of meat or in some cases without any meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and potatoes.

Al-Faham

Price : 140.Rs

Alfahm is an Arabic grilled chicken. This chicken recipe is very popular in the middle east as it is marinated with Arabian spice and barbequed in a charcoal grill or in the oven.

Shawarma

Price : 100.Rs

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern dish that originated in the Ottoman Empire, consisting of meat cut into thin slices, stacked in a cone-like shape, and roasted on a slowly-turning vertical rotisserie or spit.

Fried Rice

Price : 140.Rs

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.
